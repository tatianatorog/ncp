    Who we are

    Nadeer Capital Partners is a private equity firm specializing in buyouts and majority control investments in North American lower middle-market businesses. With a proven track record of success, we are dedicated to partnering with exceptional companies to fuel their growth and maximize their potential.

    Why Nadeer Capital Group for Private Equity?
    1. Extensive Industry Knowledge: With a comprehensive understanding of market dynamics, we possess the insights needed to identify lucrative investment opportunities and unlock their true potential.
    2. Strategic Relationships: We have cultivated an extensive network of strategic relationships within the financial, real estate, and manufacturing sectors.
    3. Operational Excellence: We are not just passive investors; we actively collaborate with management teams to implement operational improvements and growth strategies.

    Investment Strategy

    At Nadeer Capital Partners, our investment strategy is driven by a focused approach aimed at identifying and nurturing promising businesses with strong growth potential. Our investment criteria encompass three key areas: company criteria, financial criteria, and transaction criteria.

    Company Criteria

    • Located in North America
    • Strong management team
    • Owner-run business staged
      for the next phase of growth

    Financial Criteria

    • Revenue $3 million and up
    • EBITDA $500K+
    • Strong gross margin profile
    • Stable and growing revenue

    Transaction Criteria

    • Buy-outs
    • Majority and minority control acquisitions
    • Recapitalizations
